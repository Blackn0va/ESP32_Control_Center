# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want a friendly, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of age,
body, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality,
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and welcoming.
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory remarks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Encouraging or facilitating **illegal or unauthorized** use of the tooling
  (e.g. asking for help attacking networks you do not own).

## Scope & enforcement

This applies within project spaces (issues, PRs, discussions). Maintainers may
remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code, and may ban
repeat offenders.

Report issues to the maintainer via a private GitHub advisory or a direct message.
This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
